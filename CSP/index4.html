BRAD CORNELL

My love for the photographic media goes back to my high school days where I was the only yearbook photographer student.

Black and White was the normal film back in the 70s. I worked for two studios during high school printing the yearbook portraits in the darkroom. After high school I begain studing wedding and portrait photography with three photographers that were masters of the trade.

I truly enjoy photographing high school seniors, everyone of them has a "can do" attitude.
